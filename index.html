The Problem & Solution
📋
Problem
Volunteers show up when not needed — or nobody shows up at all

Local non-profits have plenty of willing volunteers, but struggle to coordinate them. Sign-up sheets are confusing email chains or physical paper on a wall. Critical shifts go empty while others get over-staffed.

🗓️
Solution
A simple, mobile-friendly shift signup platform with smart reminders

VolunteerSync lets organisations publish shifts with role requirements. Volunteers browse and sign up via SMS or web — no account needed. Auto-reminders ensure people actually show up.

How It Works
STEP 01
🏢
Organisation publishes shifts

Coordinator creates shifts with date, time, location, number of slots, and specific roles needed (driver, kitchen, etc.).

STEP 02
📲
Volunteers browse & sign up

Volunteers get a link via WhatsApp or email. They see live slot availability and sign up in one tap — no account creation.

STEP 03
🤖
Smart matching & waitlists

If a shift is full, volunteers are added to a waitlist. If someone drops out, the next person is auto-notified.

STEP 04
🔔
Reminders & check-in

Automatic SMS reminders 24hrs and 2hrs before the shift. QR code check-in on arrival for attendance tracking.

Core Features
🗓️
Live Shift Board

Visual calendar with real-time slot counts. Coordinators see at a glance where they're under-staffed.

🤝
Role-based Matching

Volunteers list their skills (driving licence, first aid, cooking). Only relevant shifts surface for them.

📲
No-app Signup

Sign up via SMS, WhatsApp, or web link. Works for all ages and demographics — especially important for older volunteers.

📊
Impact Reporting

Generate volunteer hours reports for grant applications. Track reliability scores and recognise top contributors.
